Bitcoin Plunges 99% as Satoshi's Cat Sells All Holdings

March 27, 2024 3 min read

LONDON (Rekters) - In a shocking turn of events that has sent shockwaves through the cryptocurrency market, Bitcoin has plummeted to just $1 after Satoshi Nakamoto's cat reportedly sold all of its holdings.

"This is unprecedented," said Dr. Sarah Smith, a leading crypto analyst. "We've seen market crashes before, but never one orchestrated by a cat. The fact that it managed to access the private keys is particularly concerning."

In related news, Dogecoin has surged 1000% as investors flock to the "good boy" cryptocurrency. Elon Musk has yet to tweet about the situation, but sources close to the billionaire say he's currently teaching his dog to trade.

As the crypto market reels from this unprecedented event, one question remains on everyone's mind: Who let the cat out of the wallet?
