Stock Market Now Determined by Magic 8-Ball

March 30, 2024 3 min read

NEW YORK (Rekters) - In a bold move to simplify trading, the New York Stock Exchange has replaced all analysts and algorithms with a single Magic 8-Ball.

"It's surprisingly accurate," said a trader, shaking the 8-Ball to decide whether to buy or sell. "Outlook good for tech stocks today!"

The change has led to increased volatility, with the Dow Jones Industrial Average swinging wildly based on responses like "Ask again later" and "Cannot predict now."

Investors are advised to concentrate and ask again.
